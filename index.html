Paraná, Brazil

Paraná is a state located in southern Brazil, known for its strong economy, agricultural production, and cultural diversity. It is home to Curitiba, its capital city, which is recognized for urban planning and quality of life. The state also features natural attractions such as Iguaçu Falls, one of the largest waterfall systems in the world.
